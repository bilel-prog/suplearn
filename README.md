# Sup'Learn

A resource hub for engineering students to share and find study materials, old exams, projects, and more.

## What is this?

Sup'Learn lets engineering students upload and download course materials, exams, projects, and other study resources. You can browse everything without logging in, take quizzes to test your knowledge, track your progress, and organize your study schedule.

## Features

- Browse and download resources without logging in
- Upload your own materials (professors can approve them)
- Take quizzes and track your learning progress
- Create a revision schedule to stay organized
- Rate and comment on resources to help others
- Separate dashboards for students, professors, and admins
